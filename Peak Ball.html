How to use Peak Ball

This website helps you learn English with short lessons and quick quizzes. Here’s a simple workflow:

  • Lessons: Read vocabulary and an example, then answer the lesson quiz.
  • Quiz: Take the master quiz (5 questions).
  • Progress: Track completed lessons and quiz score.

Keyboard tip: you can switch pages using your mouse; the app doesn’t require keyboard shortcuts.
